The Challenge We're Solving

In emergency situations, traditional response systems often fail when they're needed most

Delayed Response Times

Average emergency response time is 8-12 minutes in urban areas, and up to 30+ minutes in rural locations.

Location Confusion

Victims struggle to describe their exact location, leading to precious time lost in finding them.

Communication Gaps

Limited communication between victims and responders creates uncertainty and anxiety during emergencies.

How GoRescue Works

Three simple steps to connect victims with responders in real-time

1

One-Tap Emergency Alert

Victim triggers an emergency alert with a single tap. The system automatically captures GPS location and identifies the emergency type.

2

Instant Responder Dispatch

Our system finds the nearest available responder and dispatches them immediately with precise location data and emergency details.

3

Real-Time Tracking

Both victim and responder can track each other's location in real-time, with live ETA updates and secure messaging capabilities.
